Omurice



Description

Golden omelette embraces fluffy fried rice, a canvas for ketchup swirls and playful delight. Savory secrets within, creamy layers unfold, a Japanese comfort symphony in every bite. From fluffy to crispy, tangy to sweet, omurice whispers a playful tale, a whimsical hug on a plate.


Ingredients

  • Rice: Short-grain for perfect fluffiness.
  • Eggs: Light and airy, whisked to perfection.
  • Protein: Chicken, ham, or any preferred filling, diced and cooked.
  • Onion: For a sweet and savory base.
  • Butter: Adds richness to the omelet.
  • Cooking oil: To prevent sticking.
  • Ketchup: The iconic swirl atop the fluffy egg.
  • Optional Extras: Soy sauce, oyster sauce, milk, cream, cheese, peas, other vegetables.

Steps

  1. Fried Rice Fiesta: Cook rice, then saut√© diced veggies and protein in butter or oil. Stir in ketchup, salt, and pepper for tangy goodness.
  2. Egg Embrace: Whisk eggs with milk or cream for extra fluff. Heat butter in a pan until swirling (medium-low heat is key!).
  3. Pour with Patience: Tilt the pan and slowly pour in half the egg mixture. Tilt and swirl to form a thin layer. Let edges set.
  4. Rice Reunion: Spoon hot rice onto the cooked omelette half. Fold the other half over with a spatula, gently shaping into an oval.
  5. Ketchup Canvas: Draw playful swirls of ketchup on top with a spoon. Let your inner artist out!
  6. Serve with Smiles: Slide onto a plate, garnish with parsley or chives (optional), and savor the fluffy fusion.
