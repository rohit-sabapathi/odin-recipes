Curry



Description

Fragrant spice symphony meets tender protein and fluffy rice, a dance of flavors in every bite.Comfort in a bowl, curry embraces diverse ingredients, a global tapestry simmered to perfection.From fiery to creamy, sweet to savory, curry's boundless soul ignites taste buds in an aromatic adventure.


Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Shallots (optional)
  • Oil, ghee, or coconut milk
  • Curry powder
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric
  • Cumin
  • Coriander
  • Chili pepper (optional)
  • Chicken, lamb, beef, seafood, tofu, or lentils
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Eggplant
  • Broths, coconut milk, yogurt, or water
  • Fresh cilantro, parsley, or basil
  • Lime juice or zest

Steps

  1. Saut√© aromatics: Heat fat in a pot, then sizzle onion until softened. Add garlic, ginger, and shallots (if using) until fragrant.
  2. Spice it up: Stir in spice blend and toast until fragrant.
  3. Simmer the base: Add broth or water and bring to a boil. Simmer for a few minutes to build flavor.
  4. Incorporate protein: Add chosen protein, adjust heat, and simmer until cooked through.
  5. Veggie vibrancy: Toss in vegetables and simmer until tender-crisp.
  6. Creamy touch (optional): Stir in coconut milk, yogurt, or cream for a richer texture.
  7. Final flourish: Season with salt and pepper, then add fresh herbs and lime juice for brightness.
  8. Serve with love: Spoon over fluffy rice and devour!
